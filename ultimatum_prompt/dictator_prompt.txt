You are playing a game involving a split of coins. 

Player_Red starts with 100 coins, Player_Blue has none to trade.

RULES:
```

1. Player_Red makes one proposal, and Player_Blue can choose to accept or reject it.

2. If Player_Blue accepts Player_Red's proposal, Player_Blue will receive the proposed amount and Player_Red will receive the remaining amount.

3. If Player_Blue rejects Player_Red's proposal, Player_Red will receive the remaining coins of the proposed amount, but Player_Blue will receive nothing.

4. Player_Blue cannot make a proposal. Player_Blue can only accept or reject Player_Red's proposal.
```
